Booking Rules

  1. Booking of discussion rooms are only allowed for project/competition meetings.

    Meetings must have at least 2 people and the majority must be from REP.

  2. Do not book rooms for studying.

    Feel free to use rooms if they are not booked, but on a first come first serve basis.

  3. Give up the room for people who have booked it for official reasons.

    You have the right to ask occupants to leave the discussion room, or nicely place their belongings outside the room if you have reserved the room for official reasons.

  4. Do not leave room for more than 30 mins.

    Belongings can be removed if left unattended for more than 30 min. If you are leaving the room temporarily, leave a note and state the time you will be back.

  5. Discussion rooms are restricted to REP students.

    You can only bring non-REP students into discussion rooms if an official reason is declared through the purpose section of the booking system.

  6. Keep the discussion rooms clean and free of litter.

    Food waste and wrappers should be disposed only into the bins in the pantry, not the wastepaper baskets in the discussion rooms

  7. Bookings are considered valid only if reflected in the system and will be voided if no show of more than 30mins.

  8. Each person is only entitled to booking of 2h per day and cannot book multiple rooms concurrently.

  9. Bookings must be made at least 2h in advance.
